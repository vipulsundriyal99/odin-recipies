Lasagna Flatbread Recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 1 (15 ounce) container ricotta cheese
  • 1 (8 ounce) package shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 1 (3 ounce) package Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • 1 pound sausage
  • Â½ (26 ounce) jar marinara sauce
  • 6 flatbreads

Directions:

  1. Combine ricotta cheese, 1/2 of the mozzarella cheese,
    Parmesan cheese, egg, and Italian seasoning in a bowl.
  2. Cook sausage in a skillet over medium heat until no longer pink,
    5 to 10 minutes; drain. Stir in marinara sauce.
  3. Spread 1/6 of the cheese mixture evenly on each flatbread;
    cover with sausage mixture. Top with remaining mozzarella cheese.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted and bubbly, 10 to 15 minutes.
