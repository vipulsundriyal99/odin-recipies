Pasta Pomodoro Recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 1 (16 ounce) package angel hair pasta
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • ½ onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1 (10.75 ounce) can low-sodium chicken broth
  • crushed red pepper to taste
  • freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8
    minutes or until al dente; drain.
  2. Pour olive oil in a large deep skillet over high-heat. Saute onions and garlic until
    lightly browned. Reduce heat to medium-high and add tomatoes, vinegar and chicken broth;
    simmer for about 8 minutes.
  3. Stir in red pepper, black pepper, basil and cooked pasta, tossing thoroughly with
    sauce. Simmer for about 5 more minutes and serve topped with grated cheese.
