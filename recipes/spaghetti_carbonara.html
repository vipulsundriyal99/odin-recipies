Spaghetti Alla Carbonara Tradizionali

Ingredients:

  • 1 (14 ounce) package spaghetti
  • 5 ounces guanciale (cured pork cheek), cut into small cubes
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1 whole egg
  • Â¾ cup grated Pecorino-Romano cheese
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil. Stir in spaghetti and return to a boil.Cook pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 12 minutes.
    Reserve a little pasta water, then drain.
  2. Meanwhile, heat a skillet over medium heat; add guanciale, cook and stir until crisp, 5 to 10 minutes.
  3. Whisk egg yolks and whole egg together in a bowl; whisk in cheese, salt, and pepper until combined.
  4. Stir in guanciale. Add spaghetti; toss until evenly coated.
